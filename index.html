Greetings! I'm Richard A. Rojas-Rangel, also known as "S0UNDB1T3" in the digital world. I'm currently a student pursuing a BFA in Digital Media Art at San Jose State University. Art has always been my preferred medium of self-expression, and I believe it serves as a means to communicate life stories through multimedia. I adore creating various forms of digital media, be it music, art, photography, social media content, videography, or streaming. Additionally, I was born and raised in Salinas, California, and I feel a deep connection with nature and the ocean's beauty. The power and majesty of these natural wonders never cease to draw me to them. In my free time, I enjoy exploring and experiencing the beauty of the great outdoors.
Aside from my passion for art, I have a dream goal to work as a concept or model artist for Respawn Entertainment or become an animator for both 2D and 3D media. I find the prospect of creating visually stunning and immersive worlds that players can explore and experience fascinating.
For now, I continue to take art commissions through my social media pages, and I'm eager to continue my journey in digital media art while exploring opportunities to work in the gaming and animation industry.
Overall, I am a passionate and creative individual who is dedicated to pursuing my dreams while staying true to my artistic roots. I look forward to seeing where my journey in digital media art takes me and what opportunities the future holds.
