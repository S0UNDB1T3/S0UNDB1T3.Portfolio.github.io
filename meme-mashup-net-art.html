Meme Cultural Mash-up & GitHub HTML Net Art

One of the most interesting things about memes is how quickly they can spread and how rapidly they can die out. Some memes are ephemeral, lasting only a few hours or days before disappearing into obscurity. Others, however, have a longer lifespan, becoming part of our cultural lexicon and enduring for years or even decades.
In my work exploring the culture of memes, I have sought to capture the essence of this phenomenon, examining the ways in which they reflect and shape our collective consciousness. One project that stands out in my mind was my exploration of the memes surrounding Rihanna's halftime performance, which was staged to resemble the stages from the popular video game Super Smash Brothers.
Using photo editing software, I manipulated an image of the performance to include characters from the game, creating a hybrid image that merged the worlds of pop music and gaming. The resulting image was a fascinating commentary on the ways in which our cultural touchstones intersect and overlap,

GitHub HTML Net Art : Goku vs. Freiza

GitHub Link
For my Art 74 project, I decided to create a small retelling of the epic battle between Goku and Frieza from the popular anime series Dragon Ball Z. Using HTML, I highlighted the emotional and powerful speech given by the Saiyan warriors as they fought for their lives and the fate of the universe.
To bring this story to life, I incorporated a variety of different multimedia elements, including GIFs and PNG formats, creating a dynamic and engaging visual experience that drew viewers into the action. The result was a unique and exciting work of art that pushed the boundaries of traditional storytelling and showcased the power of HTML as a new form of media.
Overall, my work in exploring the potential of HTML as a new form of media has been a rewarding and exciting journey. By harnessing the power of technology and digital media, I am able to create immersive and engaging experiences that push the boundaries of traditional art forms, highlighting the potential of this unique and powerful medium.
