Glitch Art 

As an artist, my passion lies in exploring the boundaries between mediums and techniques, pushing the limits of traditional forms and experimenting with new methods to create thought-provoking and visually stimulating pieces. Glitch art has been one of my primary areas of focus, as it offers endless possibilities for manipulating digital images and sound waves to create surreal and captivating visual experiences.
My process involves taking images and converting them into sound wave data, using audio data manipulation techniques to add filters, change lengths, and corrupt the image data. This approach allows me to create unexpected and unpredictable visual effects, as the distorted audio data translates into corrupted image data.
In addition to audio data manipulation, I also experiment with using notepad to manipulate the image data directly, converting pixels into words and then adding, deleting, and manipulating that data to create unique visual effects. This process is both intuitive and experimental, as the resulting images are often unpredictable and surprising, each piece being a unique expression of the chaos and unpredictability of digital manipulation.
Despite the seemingly random nature of my approach, there is a clear intention behind each piece. By corrupting and manipulating the original image data, I am exploring the themes of impermanence, entropy, and the inherent instability of digital media. The resulting works are often surreal and abstract, yet they convey a powerful message about the fleeting nature of digital existence and the ever-evolving landscape of technology.
To complete the process, I export the transformed images back into their original files by opening them in Photoshop and creating stable PNG or JPG versions. The final product is a fascinating mix of traditional and experimental techniques, offering a unique perspective on the intersection between art and technology.
Overall, my approach to glitch art and audio data manipulation is all about experimentation and exploration. By pushing the boundaries of traditional digital art forms, I am able to create pieces that are both visually stunning and conceptually meaningful, offering a fresh perspective on the ever-evolving world of digital media.
